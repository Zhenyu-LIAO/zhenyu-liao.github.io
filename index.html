Bio

Zhenyu Liao is a second year Ph.D. student, under the supervision of Prof. Romain Couillet and Prof. Yacine Chitour, with the Signals and Statistics group of Laboratoire des signaux et systèmes, CentraleSupélec, University Paris-Saclay. He received his B.Sc degree in Optical & Electronic Information from Huazhong University of Science & Technology, China and his M.Sc. degree in Signal and Image Processing from CentraleSupélec/Paris-Sud University, France in 2016.
